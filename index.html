WHAT I CAN DO FOR YOU

  • Obtain a balance between work and life.
  • Help you identify work/life goals.
  • identify any negative attitudes you

TOOLS I USE:

SHARED TO-DO LIST

SHARED TO-DO LIST
Description

It's important to formulate a To-Do List in order
to have a clear list of goals and prioritize them.
This can be seen as a contract between the Life Coach
and the client.


JOURNALLING

JOURNALLING
Description

Regular journaling is vital to record your progress
and to identify any areas that need improvement.
A shared journal can be used to share thoughts, feelings
in real-time and can improve the bond between them.


OPEN-ENDED QUESTIONS

OPEN-ENDED QUESTIONS
Description

Sometimes more information is needed to
assess the client's current situation.
Open-ended questions is also useful to guage
the client's communication skills.


QUESTIONAIRES

QUESTIONAIRES
Description

Questionnaires can be used by the Life Coach to
guage the effectiveness of the sessions and make
any changes where necessary. A Questionnaire
consists of both open ended questions and closed
ended questions.


STRESS MANAGEMENT

STRESS MANAGEMENT
Description

We all experience stress on a regular basis.
The key is not to let it get in the way of your
goals. Stress management is vital in order to cope
with life's challenges.I can provide you with necessary
coping mechanismsto deal with stress.
